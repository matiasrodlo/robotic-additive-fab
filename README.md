A technical exploration of additive manufacturing systems integrating design, digital workflows, and fabrication. It includes FDM, VAT photopolymerization, and large-scale manufacturing (LSAM/LFAM) using pellet and filament extrusion, alongside robotic fabrication with multi-axis industrial arms (KUKA), extruders, and heated build platforms. Materials include recycled thermoplastics (LDPE, HDPE, PP, PET), mineral-filled composites, clay, resins, and low-temperature metals. Workflows combine parametric design, digital modeling, toolpath generation, and robotic fabrication to prototype and evaluate material behavior, fabrication parameters, and design outcomes.
